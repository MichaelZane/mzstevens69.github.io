About Me

My journey into web development.

The Beginning

I've always had a curious fascination with computers and what they can do. I dabbled with various aspects of computer science but never really jumped in with both feet. I was always the friend who people asked, "Can you fix my computer?" At times I would inspect elements in developer tools and wonder what it all meant. Then a wonderful opportunity through Lambda School came to light. I can pursue my passion for this life long learning adventure.

Into the fray

So my education begins at Lambda school. You are put into the fast-paced world of modern computing and navigation of web development. It was an intense journey through informational videos and 1 on 1 reviews and Timed tests. To learn is to grow and I can tell you. I have grown. I emerged on the otherside ready for what the world of web development has to offer, but I know the journey has just begun.

The Quest

So it begins, the quest, where I am allowing myself to open up to companies and letting them know the who, what, when, and how. So that I can be placed in the appropriate role where my growth can continue.
